Hey there.

This is an archival page for the contents of a brief case found at 40.7265° N, 73.9815° W Earth.

Briefcase was found at 11:41AM October 25, 2148 by a volunteer at Tompkins Square Park.

Briefcase is assumed to date back to early 2000s — most likely the 2020s. Relics belonged to a student in her early twenties.

Contents of the brief case include 5 cotton shirts, map of Earth, and speaker.

Since the date of initial discovery, additional shirts owned by same individual have been found in nearby locations.


This archival site is an effort in part of a larger collective to document vintage pieces before synthetic methods of production became the standard.

T-shirts have been cataloged based on their material, print, and production origin.

All shirts are made of non-synthetic materials, depict images of animals/environments local to Earth, and provide us background on country specific traits regarding production and culture.
